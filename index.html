Welcome to Green Bird, the internet's largest birding community with thousands of members from all over the world. The forums are dedicated to wild birds, birding, binoculars and equipment and all that goes with it.

About Our Work

The Green Bird Society aims to network birdwatchers and photographers alike by organising regular birdwatching trips to reserves and hotspots. Becoming a member will allow you to meet like-minded people whilst experiencing new animals and places, and expanding the limits to your knowledge within the world of birding. This society has a place for everyone who wants to explore their interests and the amazing nature that surrounds us, by seeing things they've never noticed before or by appreciating the ordinary.
